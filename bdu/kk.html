"Bis Auf Weiteres Eine Demonstration"
"Until further notice a demonstration"

A project in public space by Barking Dogs United
K&K Kiosk, Weimar, Germany
November 2007

In the Kiosk series "Coming Soon" 2007 in which artists were invited to recall supposed possibilities of the kiosks future, Barking Dogs United have created a piece which demonstrate the Kiosk as a demonstration. With a police "do not cross the line" tape, they have covered the Kiosk and denied access to it. Reactions towards it were: "is this for real?" whereas the Kiosk itself hasn´t commited any offence yet. Accidentally, this action took place parallel to the German train strikes.
